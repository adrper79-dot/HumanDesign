Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Prime Self for free?

Yes. Chart calculations are always free with no account required. You get 1 AI synthesis per month and daily transit briefs on the Free plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Payments are processed securely — we never store your card details.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All subscriptions are month-to-month (or annual) with no long-term commitment. Cancel from your account settings and keep access through the end of your billing period.

What's the difference between Individual and Practitioner?

Individual is for personal exploration — AI profiles, transit tools, and PDF exports. Practitioner adds client management, branded reports, session prep AI, and a public directory listing for your practice.

Do one-time purchases expire?

No. One-time purchases give you permanent credits that never expire. Use them whenever you're ready.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your chart stays on our servers. Generating your AI synthesis and sending SMS transit alerts requires trusted processors (Anthropic for AI, Telnyx for SMS) who see only the data needed to complete that task. We never sell your data or use it for advertising. Privacy Policy →
