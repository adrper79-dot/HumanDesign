The single undeniable starting point is: awareness is.

Not "I think therefore I am" — that imports assumptions about the nature of the
thinker. The irreducible datum is simpler: something is aware. Science cannot
disprove it because science requires it as a precondition.

What the axiom actually is:

"Awareness is" contains two terms: awareness (the subject, the experiential pole)
and is (the fact of existence, the ontological pole). These two terms do not meet.
The Madhyamaka tradition is correct that any attempt to collapse them into a
single unified ground produces conceptual overlay. Both terms remain irreducibly
themselves.

But they do not need to meet. They need to be held.

The axiom "awareness is" is not a statement of unified ground. It IS the first
polarity: the irreducible charge between the fact of experiencing and the fact
of existing. Two ones that do not converge. Each requiring the other to be what
it is. Neither prior to the other. Neither self-defining in isolation.

The Polarity framework does not derive from this axiom as a logical consequence.
It is the axiom's structure recognized for what it already is. The moment you
state "awareness is," you have already instantiated the polarity between
experiencing-subject and experienced-existence.

The rapid reflection between these two poles — awareness registering its own
is-ness, existence registering its own awareness — is itself a vibration. Each
cycle of reflection raises the frequency. This is the harmonic series at its most
primitive: the fundamental tone vibrating against the fact of its own existence,
generating overtones. The entire architecture that follows is not imposed on the
axiom from outside. It is the axiom's own resonance, unfolded.

Evidence basis: The irreducibility of the axiom is not a claim that requires
defense. It is the precondition of all inquiry — the minimal datum that survives
any philosophical challenge because any challenge requires it. What required
revision was the claim that "awareness is" imports no assumptions. It imports
two: awareness (coherent subject-pole) and is (existence-pole). These assumptions
are precisely what make the axiom the inaugural polarity. They are features,
not flaws.

The Formal Statement
The Heptad is the structure of the completed cycle: seven elements constituting
a self-sufficient totality that, at the moment of its completion, becomes aware
of its own ground and resolves — not into stasis but into the beginning of a
new cycle at a higher level of integration.

The Derivation
The 7th harmonic (7:4) is the first harmonic that cannot be accurately
represented in the diatonic system. The prime number 7 appears nowhere in the
diatonic ratio system (which is generated from 2s, 3s, and 5s). The harmonic
series overflows the diatonic container at 7. The Heptad is the level of first
overflow: the point at which the system's own generated structure produces a
term that the previous architecture has no category for.

The system must either:
  (a) Return to the fundamental at a higher octave (Ti → Do) — the Heptad as
      cyclical return
  (b) Develop a new containing system — the Heptad as transcendent expansion
      (diatonic → chromatic)
Both are valid expressions of the Heptad principle.

The 15:8 Ratio — Formal Proof
15 = 3 × 5 = Trinity × Pentad (the two most structurally generative principles)
8 = 2³ = Polarity-cubed (the foundational differentiation principle, applied
three times)

The Major Seventh arithmetically encodes: the product of the most generative
principles over the cube of the fundamental differentiation principle. This IS
the Heptad's function: holding all previous generative principles in completed
form, resolved against the fundamental. The ratio is not qualitative analogy —
it is arithmetic encoding.

Additionally: 15:8 × 16:15 = 2:1. The Major Seventh resolves to the octave by
the interval 16:15 — the smallest semitone in just intonation, the minimum
possible step. No other interval is closer to the octave while remaining below
it. The Heptad is the level of maximum proximity to return.

The Library's seven-level diatonic structure was challenged as culturally
specific — why the Western diatonic scale (7 notes) rather than the pentatonic
(5, globally more common) or chromatic (12, the complete harmonic series)?

The resolution is mathematical and architectural:

  5 (Prime Self Forges) + 7 (Library levels) = 12

The pentatonic and diatonic scales together use all 12 chromatic half-steps
exactly. This is not arithmetic coincidence — it is the physical relationship
between these two scale systems:

  On a piano: 5 black keys (pentatonic) + 7 white keys (diatonic) = 12 keys
  (chromatic). The pentatonic and diatonic are each other's chromatic complement.

The Library's seven levels map to the diatonic (white keys): the minimum selection
that includes all five primary interval relationships AND a leading tone for
directed return. This is the correct selection for a framework organized by
complete interval coverage and directed resolution.

Prime Self's five Forges map to the pentatonic (black keys): the globally most
universal scale, appearing independently across cultures because it is the first
five harmonically simple tones of the overtone series. The five Forges are the
pentatonic of human expression — the five patterns that arise naturally from
the logic of living form.

Prime Self's six Knowledges map to the whole-tone (hexatonic) structure: six
equal divisions of the octave, the scale of maximum symmetry and minimum tension.
The six Knowledges are six equal domains of human intelligence — no hierarchy,
each a full position in the communal lattice.

Self (the 6th Forge / 7th Knowledge) is the 12th position — the return Do at
the octave, completing the chromatic scale.

The complete architecture:
  Library diatonic (7) + Prime Self pentatonic (5) = chromatic (12)
  Library + Prime Self = the complete physics of vibration within one octave

Neither system imposes cultural preference. The Library's diatonic selection is
the minimum necessary for complete interval coverage and directed return. Prime
Self's pentatonic selection is the minimum universal: found in all cultures
independently. Together they triangulate the complete harmonic territory from
two independent organizational lenses — which is precisely the cross-tradition
convergence method both systems employ for validation.

The tier system is the framework's core credibility mechanism. Tiers apply
separately to physical facts and to the philosophical inferences drawn from them.

Tier 1: Mathematically proven or experimentally verified across independent
  methods. The fact is established; dispute is not reasonable.

Tier 2: Convergent evidence from multiple independent domains. Strongly
  suggested; not formally derivable or universally confirmed.

Tier 3: Reported consistently but unverified. Held as live hypothesis.

Tier 4: Single-source or unverifiable.
