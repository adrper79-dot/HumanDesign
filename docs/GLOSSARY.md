A comprehensive reference for all technical terms, jargon, and acronyms used in Prime Self. Organized by category for easy navigation.

## Energy Blueprint Terms

### Core Concepts

**Energy Blueprint**  
Your personal energy map calculated from your exact birth time and location. Shows how you're designed to interact with the world, make decisions, and fulfill your purpose. Previously known by trademarked system names.

**Pattern** (formerly "Type")  
Your fundamental energy operating system. Determines how you engage with life and others. Five patterns exist: Builder, Guide, Initiator, Builder-Initiator, and Reflector.

**Decision Style** (formerly "Authority")  
Your inner guidance system for making aligned choices. Examples include Emotional Wave Navigation (waiting through emotional cycles), Sacral Response (gut instinct), Splenic Instinct (in-the-moment knowing), Heart Will (self-direction), and Environmental Reflection (sampling others' energy).

**Strategy**  
Your interaction approach with the world. How you initiate (or don't initiate) actions. Examples: "To Respond" (wait for external prompts), "Wait for Invitation" (be recognized first), "Inform Before Acting" (communicate your plans), "Wait a Lunar Cycle" (sample energy for 28 days).

**Life Role** (formerly "Profile")  
Your personality archetype expressed as two numbers (e.g., 6/2, 1/3, 4/6). The first number is your conscious role (how others see you), the second is unconscious (how you see yourself). Twelve possible combinations define how you learn, relate, and evolve.

**Purpose Vector** (formerly "Incarnation Cross")  
Your life's thematic purpose formed by the four main gene key activations: conscious Sun/Earth and unconscious Sun/Earth. Named after the conscious Sun position (e.g., "Right Angle Cross of the Sphinx"). Indicates your unique contribution to the world.

**Gene Keys** (also "Gates")  
The 64 energy points in your chart, numbered 1-64. Each corresponds to a hexagram from the I Ching and a specific gene in your DNA. Can be "defined" (colored, consistent) or "undefined" (white, variable). Active gene keys create your energetic signature.

**Centers**  
The nine bio-energy hubs in your chart: Head, Ajna, Throat, G, Heart (Will), Solar Plexus, Sacral, Spleen, Root. Each governs specific life themes. **Defined** centers (colored) operate consistently; **Open** centers (white) amplify and sample others' energy, making you wise about that theme.

**Channels**  
Energy pathways connecting two gene keys between centers. When both ends are defined, the channel "activates" and becomes a consistent talent or theme in your life. 36 possible channels exist.

**Circuit Type** (formerly "Definition")  
How your defined centers connect. Determines how you process and share energy:
- **Single**: All defined centers connected in one circuit (integrated flow)
- **Split**: Two separate circuits (need bridge from others)
- **Triple Split**: Three circuits (complex bridging needs)
- **Quad Split**: Four circuits (highly selective about company)

**Not-Self Theme**  
The emotional signal that you're out of alignment with your pattern and strategy. Each pattern has a specific theme: Frustration (Builder), Bitterness (Guide), Anger (Initiator), Frustration/Anger (Builder-Initiator), Disappointment (Reflector).

### The Five Patterns

**Builder** (formerly "Generator")  
~37% of population. Consistent life-force energy. Designed to respond to external prompts rather than initiate. Sacral center defined. Strategy: Wait to Respond. Not-Self: Frustration. Signature: Satisfaction.

**Guide** (formerly "Projector")  
~20% of population. Recognition-based energy. Designed to manage, guide, and direct others' energy. No motor centers defined to Sacral. Strategy: Wait for Invitation. Not-Self: Bitterness. Signature: Success.

**Initiator** (formerly "Manifestor")  
~9% of population. Self-starting, initiating energy. Designed to impact and get things moving. Throat connected to motor (not Sacral). Strategy: Inform Before Acting. Not-Self: Anger. Signature: Peace.

**Builder-Initiator** (formerly "Manifesting Generator")  
~33% of population. Hybrid multi-passionate energy. Fast-moving responder with initiating capacity. Sacral defined + motor to throat. Strategy: Wait to Respond, then Inform. Not-Self: Frustration & Anger. Signature: Satisfaction & Peace.

**Reflector**  
~1% of population. Lunar mirror energy. Designed to sample and reflect the health of communities. All centers open. Strategy: Wait a Lunar Cycle (28.5 days). Not-Self: Disappointment. Signature: Surprise.

## Astrology Terms

**Western Astrology**  
The traditional zodiac system based on 12 signs, 10 planets, 12 houses, and planetary aspects. Provides complementary insights to Energy Blueprint analysis.

**Ascendant** (Rising Sign)  
The zodiac sign rising on the eastern horizon at your birth moment. How you appear to others and approach new situations. Your social mask and first impression.

**Midheaven** (MC)  
The zodiac sign at the highest point in the sky at your birth. Represents your public image, career path, and life aspirations.

**Natal Chart**  
Your birth chart snapshot: planetary positions at your exact birth time/location. The foundation for all astrological analysis.

**Transits**  
Current planetary positions in the sky as they move through the zodiac. When today's planets interact with your natal chart, they create activating energy windows.

**Houses**  
The 12 life areas in astrology: self (1st), resources (2nd), communication (3rd), home (4th), creativity (5th), health (6th), relationships (7th), transformation (8th), philosophy (9th), career (10th), community (11th), spirituality (12th).

**Aspects**  
Angular relationships between planets. Major aspects: Conjunction (0°, merging), Opposition (180°, tension), Trine (120°, flow), Square (90°, challenge), Sextile (60°, opportunity).

**Zodiac Signs**  
The 12 archetypal energies: Aries (pioneer), Taurus (builder), Gemini (communicator), Cancer (nurturer), Leo (performer), Virgo (analyst), Libra (diplomat), Scorpio (transformer), Sagittarius (explorer), Capricorn (achiever), Aquarius (innovator), Pisces (mystic).

## Prime Self System Terms

### The Five Forges

Your Forge is your Prime Self archetype — the domain of power you're called to master. Determined deterministically by `computeForge()` using a 9-step weighted scoring algorithm from `forge_mapping.json` (type weight 10, gate indicators 3, center indicators 2, motor-to-throat 4, astro signs 2, astro houses 1).

**Canonical names** (used in code and validation): Initiation, Mastery, Guidance, Perception, Transformation  
**Archetype aliases** (used in narrative/onboarding): Chronos, Eros, Aether, Lux, Phoenix

**Initiation / Chronos (The Forge of Time)**  
Domain: Mastering past/present/future. HD Triggers: Root center definition, Saturn-related gates (38, 54, 58). Astro Triggers: Saturn aspects, Capricorn placements. Historical Exemplars: Marcus Aurelius, Nelson Mandela, Maya Angelou. Weapon: The Hourglass. Defense: Strategic Patience.

**Mastery / Eros (The Forge of Passion)**  
Domain: Mastering desire and creative fire. HD Triggers: Defined Sacral Center, Channel 59-6 (Intimacy). Astro Triggers: Venus aspects, Mars strength, Scorpio/Taurus placements. Historical Exemplars: Frida Kahlo, Pablo Neruda, Josephine Baker. Weapon: The Flame. Defense: Sustained Devotion.

**Guidance / Aether (The Forge of Connection)**  
Domain: Interconnectedness and unity consciousness. HD Triggers: G Center definition, Channel 25-51 (Initiation). Astro Triggers: Neptune aspects, 12th house emphasis, Pisces placements. Historical Exemplars: Rumi, Thich Nhat Hanh, Mother Teresa. Weapon: The Thread. Defense: Compassionate Boundaries.

**Perception / Lux (The Forge of Illumination)**  
Domain: Truth, clarity, and knowledge. HD Triggers: Ajna center definition, Head Center activation. Astro Triggers: Mercury strength, Uranus aspects, Aquarius/Gemini placements. Historical Exemplars: Leonardo da Vinci, Marie Curie, Nikola Tesla. Weapon: The Prism. Defense: Radical Honesty.

**Transformation / Phoenix (The Forge of Rebirth)**  
Domain: Transformation, renewal, and resurrection. HD Triggers: Solar Plexus definition, Channel 36-35 (Transitoriness). Astro Triggers: Pluto aspects, 8th house emphasis, Scorpio placements. Historical Exemplars: Nelson Mandela, Malcolm X, Frida Kahlo. Weapon: Ashes of the Past. Defense: Surrender to Cycles.

**Mysteries** (#6)  
Prime Question: "What lies beyond the known?" Transcendent wisdom — the discipline of surrender to the unknowable. Combines all other Knowledges into integration and wholeness.

## Technical Terms (Platform)

**API** (Application Programming Interface)  
The communication layer that lets the frontend (what you see) talk to the backend (where calculations happen). Prime Self's API handles chart calculations, profile generation, and data storage.

**Cloudflare Workers**  
The serverless platform running Prime Self's API. Code executes at edge locations worldwide for fast response times (under 100ms for chart calculations).

**Edge Computing**  
Running code close to users geographically instead of in a single data center. Reduces latency and improves performance.

**JWT** (JSON Web Token)  
The authentication system for signed-in users. A secure token proving your identity without storing passwords in memory.

**Neon PostgreSQL**  
The serverless database storing user accounts, saved charts, profiles, and practitioner data. Automatically scales and pools connections.

**SPA** (Single-Page Application)  
The frontend architecture. One HTML file loads once, then updates dynamically without page refreshes. Makes the interface feel fast and responsive.

## AI/Synthesis Terms

**LLM** (Large Language Model)  
The AI system (Claude by Anthropic) that generates personalized Prime Self Profiles. Trained on vast text to understand context and create human-like synthesis.

**Claude Opus**  
The highest-intelligence Claude model. Used for complex 8-layer profile synthesis. Handles nuance, tone, and multi-system integration.

**Claude Sonnet**  
The balanced Claude model. Used for structured data extraction and chart comparisons. Fast and accurate for defined tasks.

**Claude Haiku**  
The fastest Claude model. Used for high-volume operations like daily transit digests and simple explanations.

**RAG** (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)  
The technique where the AI pulls relevant knowledge from Prime Self's database before generating text. Ensures accurate, grounded responses instead of generic output.

**System Prompt**  
The instructions given to the AI defining its role, tone, forbidden terminology, and output structure. Prime Self's system prompt enforces IP-safe language and beginner-friendly tone.

**Token**  
The unit of text the AI processes. ~4 characters = 1 token. Longer prompts and outputs cost more tokens. Prime Self optimizes prompts to stay under budget.

## Data & Calculation Terms

**Julian Day Number (JDN)**  
The astronomical time system counting days since January 1, 4713 BCE. Used for precise planetary position calculations independent of calendars.

**UTC** (Coordinated Universal Time)  
The global time standard (formerly GMT). All birth times convert to UTC before calculation to ensure accuracy across timezones.

**IANA Timezone**  
The standard timezone database (e.g., `America/New_York`, `Europe/London`). Accounts for daylight saving time and historical timezone changes.

**Ephemeris**  
The astronomical tables showing planetary positions over time. Prime Self uses VSOP87 theory for high-precision calculations.

**Geocoding**  
Converting location names to latitude/longitude coordinates. Prime Self geocodes birth locations to calculate local horizon (Ascendant) and house positions.

## Feature-Specific Terms

### Rectification

**Birth Time Rectification**  
The process of finding your accurate birth time when it's unknown or uncertain. Analyzes how your chart changes across a time window to identify likely moments.

**Time Window Analysis**  
Calculating charts at 15-minute intervals across a range (e.g., 2-hour window) to see pattern shifts, gene key changes, and decision style variations.

### Composite Charts

**Composite**  
A combined chart for two people showing the relationship energy between them. Not averaged—created by midpoint calculations of each planetary pair.

**Electromagnetic Connection**  
The specific channels and gates that "bridge" between two charts. Shows where one person's defined energy completes the other's open centers.

### Practitioner Tools

**Practitioner Account**  
Enhanced account tier for professional chart readers. Includes client roster management, batch calculations, and session note storage.

**Client Roster**  
The list of clients linked to a practitioner account. Practitioners can access client charts and profiles while maintaining privacy boundaries.

## Usage Guidelines

### Preferred Terminology (User-Facing)

Use these beginner-friendly terms in all documentation, UI, and generated content:

- ✅ **Energy Blueprint** (not "Human Design" ™)
- ✅ **Pattern** (not "Type")
- ✅ **Decision Style** (not "Authority")
- ✅ **Life Role** (not "Profile")
- ✅ **Purpose Vector** (not "Incarnation Cross" ™)
- ✅ **Builder / Guide / Initiator / Builder-Initiator / Reflector** (avoid trademarked type names)

### Technical Documentation Exceptions

In code comments, database schemas, and internal technical docs, you may use shorthand:
- `type` (energy pattern)
- `authority` (decision style)
- `profile` (life role)
- `cross` (purpose vector)

### Forbidden Terms (Legal)

Never use these in user-facing text without attribution:
- Direct trademarked system names
- Specific trademarked type terminology
- Trademarked cross nomenclature
