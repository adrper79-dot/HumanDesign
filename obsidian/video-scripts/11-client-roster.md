# Video 11 — Client Roster

## Script

**[SCREEN: Client roster tab]**

"Your client roster is the center of your practice on Prime Self. Every client you work with lives here — their chart, your notes, your session prep."

**[CLICK: Invite Client button]**

"To add a client, click Invite Client and enter their email. Prime Self sends them an invitation to create a free account and connect with you as their practitioner."

**[SHOW: pending invitation state]**

"While the invitation is pending, you'll see it in your roster with a 'Pending' badge. You can resend the invitation or send a reminder if they haven't responded."

**[SHOW: accepted client card]**

"Once they accept, their card appears in your roster. Click their name to open their full profile — their chart, your notes, the AI synthesis."

**[SHOW: client detail view]**

"From a client's detail view you can: view their full chart, generate a session prep synthesis, add private notes only you can see, run a composite chart between you and them, and export their profile as a branded PDF."

**[SHOW: notes section]**

"Session notes are private to you — the client never sees these. Log what you discussed, what resonated, what to follow up on next session."

**[SHOW: Practitioner limit]**

"Practitioner tier supports up to 500 clients. Agency tier has no limit."
