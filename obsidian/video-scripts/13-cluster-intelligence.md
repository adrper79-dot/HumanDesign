# Video 13 — Cluster Intelligence

## Script

**[SCREEN: Clusters tab]**

"Cluster Intelligence is Prime Self's group analysis feature — synthesize the energy dynamics of a team, family, or cohort from their combined chart data."

**[CLICK: Create Cluster]**

"Click Create Cluster and name it — for example, 'Leadership Team Q2' or 'Family Group'."

**[SHOW: add members flow]**

"Add members by selecting from your existing client roster, or invite new people by email. Each member needs to have a chart in the system."

**[SHOW: cluster with 4 members]**

"With members added, you can see the group energy overview — which types are represented, what the dominant authority patterns are, where the group has defined vs. open channels collectively."

**[CLICK: Synthesize Cluster]**

"Click Synthesize Cluster to generate the AI group synthesis. This takes all members' charts and narrates the group's energy dynamics — where synergy lives, where friction is likely, and how the group makes decisions collectively."

**[SHOW: synthesis output]**

"The synthesis identifies: dominant energy patterns in the group, potential electromagnetic connections between members, collective decision-making style, and recommendations for working with the group's design."

**[HIGHLIGHT: practitioner framing]**

"This is a practitioner tool — written to help you facilitate group sessions, not to be shared verbatim with the group. Use it as your prep brief."
