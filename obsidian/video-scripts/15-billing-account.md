# Video 15 — Billing & Account

## Script

**[SCREEN: Account / Settings tab]**

"Let's walk through billing, upgrades, and account management."

**[SHOW: current tier badge]**

"Your current tier is always visible — Free, Individual, Practitioner, or Agency."

**[CLICK: Upgrade button]**

"Clicking Upgrade opens the tier selection. Individual is $19/month, Practitioner is $97/month. Annual billing saves 17%."

**[SHOW: Stripe checkout]**

"Checkout is powered by Stripe — industry-standard payment security. Prime Self never stores your card details."

**[SHOW: billing portal]**

"Once subscribed, click Manage Billing to open the Stripe billing portal — update payment method, download invoices, or change your plan."

**[SHOW: cancellation flow]**

"Cancellation is always available — no lock-in. If you're on the Practitioner tier, we'll show you a downgrade option to Individual at $19/month before confirming cancellation — preserving your account history."

**[SHOW: one-time purchases]**

"One-time purchases — single synthesis, transit pass, composite reading — are available without upgrading your subscription tier."

**[SHOW: lifetime access option]**

"Lifetime Access is $299 — one payment for Individual tier features permanently. Best value if you plan to use Prime Self long-term."
