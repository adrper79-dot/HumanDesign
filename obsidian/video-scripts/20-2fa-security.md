# Video 20 — 2FA & Security

## Script

**[SCREEN: Account settings — Security section]**

"Two-factor authentication adds a second layer of protection to your Prime Self account — especially important if you manage client data as a practitioner."

**[CLICK: Enable 2FA]**

"Click Enable Two-Factor Authentication. You'll see a QR code — scan it with any TOTP authenticator app: Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, or Bitwarden."

**[SHOW: QR code scan flow]**

"Once scanned, your authenticator generates a 6-digit code that rotates every 30 seconds. Enter the current code to confirm setup."

**[SHOW: backup codes]**

"Save your backup codes — these are one-time codes you can use if you lose access to your authenticator. Store them somewhere secure."

**[SHOW: 2FA login flow]**

"After enabling 2FA, login requires your email, password, and the 6-digit code from your authenticator — in that order."

**[SHOW: data export option]**

"In Account Settings you can also request a full data export — all your charts, profiles, diary entries, and assessments as a JSON file. Your data, always."
