# Video 17 — Push Notifications

## Script

**[SCREEN: Notifications settings]**

"Push notifications let Prime Self alert you to significant transit events — without SMS, directly in your browser or as a PWA on mobile."

**[CLICK: Enable Push Notifications]**

"Click Enable — your browser will ask for notification permission. Allow it."

**[SHOW: alert types]**

"You can configure alerts for: daily transit summary, major planetary movements through your defined gates, and custom alerts you set up in the Alerts section."

**[SHOW: custom alert creation]**

"Custom alerts let you pick a specific planet and gate combination — 'notify me when Saturn enters Gate 38'. Useful if you're tracking a specific transit cycle."

**[SHOW: delivery history]**

"Your push notification delivery history is logged — you can see what was sent and when, and replay any alert you missed."

**[SHOW: PWA install prompt]**

"For the best push notification experience, install Prime Self as a PWA — click the install prompt in your browser address bar. This puts it on your home screen with full notification support."
