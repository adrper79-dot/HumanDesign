# Video 14 — Branded PDFs

## Script

**[SCREEN: Client detail → Export]**

"Practitioners can export a fully branded PDF for any client — a professional deliverable you can send after a reading session."

**[CLICK: Export Branded PDF]**

"Click Export Branded PDF. Prime Self generates a multi-page document: chart overview, AI synthesis, transit snapshot, assessment results if available."

**[SHOW: PDF preview]**

"The PDF is branded with the Prime Self design system — clean, professional, dark theme. You can include your practitioner name on the cover page."

**[SHOW: customization options]**

"Future versions will support custom logo and color theme upload — that's the Agency tier white-label capability currently in development."

**[SHOW: delivery options]**

"Download and send via email, or share the direct link. The PDF is stored in cloud storage — the link stays live for 30 days."

**[SHOW: client PDF vs practitioner PDF]**

"Note: the branded PDF is the client-facing version — it does not include your private session notes. Those stay in your practitioner view only."
